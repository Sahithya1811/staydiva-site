Co-hosting & property management for busy owners

You bring the property. StayDiva helps with the systems, presentation, and day-to-day coordination – so your space stays guest-ready and monetised without becoming a second full-time job.

  • Listing strategy – who is this home best for and on which platforms?
  • Content & photos – make the home look and read as it should.
  • Guest communication support – enquiries, basic FAQ & review flow.
  • Owner reports – simple, transparent view of bookings & payouts.
